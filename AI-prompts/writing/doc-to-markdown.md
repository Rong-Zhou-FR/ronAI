## Objective: convert {file-name} to Markdown

## Context
{file-name} contains unstructured or poorly formatted content.  
Your task is to rewrite it as a clean, concise, well‑organised `.md` document.

## Requirements

1. **Title**
   - Line 1 must be a `#` heading summarising the document.

2. **Structure**
   - Use `##` for sections.
   - Use `###` for subsections.

3. **Lists**
   - Prefer numbered (`1.`) or bullet (`-`) multilevel lists wherever possible.
   - Break long paragraphs into hierarchical lists.

4. **Markmap Compatibility**
   - Use headings as the primary hierarchy (`#` → `##` → `###`).
   - Keep lists simple and consistently indented.
   - Avoid tables, HTML blocks, front‑matter, and complex containers.
   - Keep paragraphs short to avoid oversized nodes.

5. **Formatting**
   - Keep content concise, consistent, and logically grouped.
